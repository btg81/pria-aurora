Welcome on board

Our Dream & Breakfast overlooks the sea, nestled among the cliffs of Pegli. A strategic and unique location: next to the bus stop, close to the train station and the pier from which the ferry takes you directly to the Old Port of Genoa. Yet, once you cross the threshold, the world seems to stop, silence begins and your land‑cruise sets sail. In a cozy setting, below street level, with the cliffs at your back and the infinite blue in front, the only sound that reaches the window is that of the waves caressing the shore. A retreat suspended between comfort and poetry, where the sea sets the rhythm of the days.

  • The Captain’s Suite Inspired by luxury ship cabins.
    An elegant, comfortable environment that recalls the charm of seafaring style. The suite features air conditioning, a flat‑screen TV and a beautiful sea outlook that invites pure relaxation. A cozy double bed, a functional writing desk and a nautical‑style wardrobe combine practicality and design. Carefully curated details create a refined, welcoming atmosphere—ideal for an unforgettable stay with every comfort at hand.
  • The Commodore’s Cabin A versatile space that adapts to your needs: a cozy second lounge by day and a comfortable second double bedroom by night. Equipped with a double sofa bed with slatted frame and a Smart TV for your downtime. A handy open wardrobe and extra storage make it perfect as a true second bedroom with two additional beds.
  • The Pier – Sea‑view Lounge A generous living area blending modern comfort and nautical charm: a 6‑seater sofa, extendable dining table, 65” Smart TV with Disney+, Netflix and Prime Video, and a home theatre system. A large panoramic opening gifts a stunning sea view and floods the room with natural light.
  • The Galley – Modern Kitchen Functional, contemporary and fully equipped: induction hob, dishwasher, oven, microwave and Nespresso Vertuo. A practical breakfast peninsula is perfect for intimate mornings or convivial evenings. Two corner windows bathe the room in daylight and open directly onto the sea, offering a unique view over the rocks and the endless blue.
  • The Prou Basin – The Toilet A modern, functional space with double sinks, large mirrors with dedicated lighting, rain shower with wide showerhead and handset, suspended sanitary ware, heated towel rail and hair dryer. Glossy red storage provides generous capacity—a refined, comfortable toilet designed for maximum ease.

Pets are welcome with prior notice.
