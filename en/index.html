Welcome on board

Our Dream & Breakfast overlooks the sea, nestled among the cliffs of Pegli. A strategic and unique location: next to the bus stop, close to the train station and the pier from which the ferry takes you directly to the Old Port of Genoa. Yet, once you cross the threshold, the world seems to stop, silence begins and your land-cruise sets sail. In a cozy setting, below street level, with the cliffs at your back and the infinite blue in front, the only sound that reaches the window is that of the waves caressing the shore. A retreat suspended between comfort and poetry, where the sea sets the rhythm of the days.

  • The Captain’s Suite Inspired by luxury ship cabins. An elegant and comfortable environment that recalls the charm of seafaring style. The suite is equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and a charming sea view. It features a comfortable double bed, a functional writing desk and a nautical-style wardrobe, combining practicality and design. The carefully curated decor creates a refined and welcoming atmosphere, ideal for an unforgettable stay with all the comforts at hand.
  • The Commodore’s Cabin A versatile space that transforms as needed: during the day it serves as a second lounge, perfect for relaxation or reading, while at night it becomes a second double bedroom, intimate and comfortable. Equipped with a practical sofa bed with frame and a Smart TV, the cabin is designed to provide comfort and functionality. A handy open wardrobe and spacious storage units complete the room, making it ideal as an additional bedroom with two extra beds. A cozy retreat, perfect both as an extension of the living area and to host family or friends.
  • The Pier – Sea View Lounge A spacious living area combining modern comfort and maritime charm. Furnished with a 6-seater sofa, an extendable dining table, a 65” Smart TV with Disney+, Netflix and Prime Video, and a home theatre system, it is the ideal space for convivial moments. A large panoramic opening offers a breathtaking sea view, where the blue merges with the natural light flooding the space.
  • The Galley – Modern Kitchen A functional, contemporary design, fully equipped with induction hob, dishwasher, oven, microwave and Nespresso Vertuo machine. A practical breakfast island makes it perfect both for intimate breakfasts and convivial dinners. Two corner windows flood the space with natural light and open directly onto the sea, offering a unique view of the cliffs and the infinite blue. An environment that blends practicality and poetry, where every detail is designed to make your experience memorable.
  • The Bathroom – The Toilette A modern, functional space with double sink, large mirrors with dedicated lighting, rain shower with large showerhead and hand shower, wall-mounted sanitary ware, heated towel rack and hairdryer. Featuring glossy red cabinets with ample storage space, this refined and welcoming bathroom is designed for maximum comfort.

Pets are welcome, upon prior notice.
