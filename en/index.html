Welcome aboard

Our Dream & Breakfast overlooks the sea, nestled among the rocks of Pegli. A strategic and unique location: next to the bus stop, close to the train station, and to the pier where the ferry takes you directly to the Porto Antico of Genoa. Yet, once you cross the threshold, the world goes silent. In a cosy setting, located below street level, with rocks at the back and the endless blue in front, the only sound that reaches the window is that of the waves caressing the shore. A refuge suspended between comfort and poetry, where the sea sets the pace of the day.

  • Master suite inspired by luxury ship cabins: air conditioning, TV, and enchanting sea view.
  • Living area with lounge and open kitchen: large 6-seater sofa, 65” Smart TV with Disney+, Netflix and Prime Video, home theatre system, extendable dining table and breakfast island. Three panoramic windows flood the space with light and open directly onto the sea (with the possibility to hang laundry in the typical Genoese style, overlooking the water).
  • Second bedroom with double sofa bed (total 4 beds), air conditioning and Smart TV.
  • Spacious bathroom with double sinks, suspended fixtures, and modern rain shower.

Pets are welcome upon prior notice.
