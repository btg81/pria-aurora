Welcome aboard

Our Dream & Breakfast sits right on the sea, nestled among Pegli’s rocks. A strategic yet peaceful location: bus stop, train station and the pier to the Old Port are just minutes away. Below street level, with only the blue in front. From the window, the only sound is the waves.

  • High‑speed Wi‑Fi
  • Full kitchen: induction, dishwasher, oven, microwave, Nespresso Vertuo
  • Extendable dining table and breakfast island
  • Sea view with three windows right over the rocks
  • Bathroom with double sinks, wall‑hung toilets and rain shower
  • Pet‑friendly upon notice
